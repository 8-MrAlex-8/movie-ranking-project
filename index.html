Series are a way to engage one's imagination, and fuel one's fantasy. It is a portal to an endless maze of art, full of scenes which stroke the mind and impact the heart.

Whether it's the thrill of action, some spine-chilling horror, the lighthearted joy of comedy, or the heartfelt moments of love, series and shows hold a special place in everyone's heart. This project is a personal exploration of my favorite ones, spanning genres and eras, each of which has left a big mark on my life.

Join me as I celebrate these cinematic gems that have inspired, entertained, and moved me in countless ways.

Regular Show
Regular Show is undeniably my most favorite one out of this list. It is a (quite-PG) cartoon series following the lives of Mordecai, Rigby, and their friends at the park where they work at. The show follows a formula of mischief, scientific fantasies which boggles the mind, and at least one count of their boss Benson threatening to fire them.
The Amazing World of Gumball
The world of this cartoon--no pun intended--is very rich and vibrant. Every character has their own charms and depth, enabling a sweet ride back to one's childhood. Although the show's vibes are very apparently for kids, the show sports some crude humor that a child can only understand once they're older.
Money Heist
The first real-life crime drama series in this list follows a band of thieves who come together under the guidance of "The Professor" to pull off some of the most ambitious heists in history. Driven by rebellion and greed, their journey is chock-full of guns blazing, smart plans, and heart-stopping twists. With a striking musical theme, especially the iconic Bella Ciao, this drama offers viewers an intense package of mystery, pain, passion, and raw emotion.

The Disastrous Life of Saiki K.
This hilarious slice-of-life anime takes you through a psychic--named Saiki Kusuo--and his attempts to lead a normal life despite his (admittedly-overpowered) abilities. He also gains some friends throughout the show who, altogether, lights its audience up with their dynamic. It's a great humor-filled series that's also quite immersive.
Hell's Kitchen
At this point, who hasn't heard of Gordon Ramsay? His cooking show Hell's Kitchen has been a childhood favorite of mine. Chefs from all walks of life battle it out in a cutthroat kitchen for the chance to achieve their dreams. From prep to dinner service, along with Gordon's signature temper, Hell's Kitchen presents its watchers with a solid mix of shouting intensity and culinary art.
Asobi Asobase
At first glance, Asobi Asobase seems like an innocent comedy about three schoolgirls forming a club to pass time. But, it is anything but ordinary. The trio's misadventures are packed with unexpected, absurd humor that keeps even its loyal watchers guessing! With exaggerated reactions and bizarre situations, it's a rollercoaster of chaos and comedy that will leave you in stitches. The ridiculousness of it all makes Asobi Asobase one of the series that I religiously rewatch.

Kakegurui
Welcome to Hyakkaou Private Academy, where gambling isn't just a pastime—it's everything. Students risk their fortunes, reputations, and futures in high-stakes games that could make or break them. All until Yumeko Jabami, a seemingly sweet girl with a shocking love for the thrill of gambling, comes to play. With its stunning animation, intense psychological battles, and magnificent drama, Kakegurui is a wild ride that explores amazing twists in the game, with a flavor of showing just how much people are willing to gamble away.
Kingdom
Set in Korea’s Joseon Dynasty, Kingdom blends historical drama with a gripping, blood-curdling twist. When a mysterious plague begins turning people into ravenous monsters, Crown Prince Lee Chang must uncover the truth behind the epidemic while battling political conspiracies that threaten his throne. With its breathtaking cinematography, intense action, and intricate plot, Kingdom delivers a chilling tale of survival, power, and humanity amidst an undead apocalypse. It’s a perfect mix of suspense, horror, and historical intrigue that will leave you hooked.
Alice in Borderland
Imagine ending up in a deserted Tokyo where it seems as if you were the most-first one there--that's the terrifying reality in Alice in Borderland. This show sports some of the most amazing games Arisu and his friends end up in, games where intelligence, strength, teamwork, and the will to betray are the only ways to survive. With what it delivers, this show pulls you in from the first moment and doesn't let go. It's a heart-pounding journey through a world where survival is the ultimate prize.
